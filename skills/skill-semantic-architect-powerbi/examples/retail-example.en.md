## 1. Industry Overview

### Industry: Retail

The retail industry encompasses the sale of products directly to end consumers through physical and digital channels. Data analysis in retail focuses on optimizing sales, managing inventory, understanding customer behavior, and maximizing profit margins.

### Key Analytical Challenges

- **Seasonality**: Identifying and predicting demand peaks (Black Friday, holidays, back-to-school)
- **Omnichannel**: Unifying metrics across physical stores, e-commerce, and marketplaces
- **Margin by product**: Distinguishing between high-volume/low-margin vs. low-volume/high-margin products

### Detected Business Context

Contoso Sales model with classic star schema structure: 1 fact table (sales) surrounded by product, customer, store, date, and geography dimensions. Model prepared for multidimensional analysis with natural hierarchies.

## 3. KPI Catalog

### Proposed Measures

#### Total Revenue

```dax
[Total Revenue] =
    SUMX(
        FactSales,
        FactSales[Quantity] * FactSales[UnitPrice]
    )
```

- **Justification**: Total gross revenue before discounts. Fundamental retail metric.
- **Business Question**: "What was the total revenue for the period?"
- **Category**: Revenue
- **Format**: $#,0

#### Gross Margin %

```dax
[Gross Margin %] =
    DIVIDE(
        [Total Revenue] - [Total Cost],
        [Total Revenue],
        0
    )
```

- **Justification**: Gross profit percentage. Key profitability indicator.
- **Business Question**: "What profit margin are we achieving?"
- **Category**: Profitability
- **Format**: 0.0%

#### Total Cost

```dax
[Total Cost] =
    SUMX(
        FactSales,
        FactSales[Quantity] * RELATED(DimProduct[UnitCost])
    )
```

- **Justification**: Total cost of goods sold.
- **Business Question**: "How much did the sold inventory cost us?"
- **Category**: Cost
- **Format**: $#,0

#### Units Sold

```dax
[Units Sold] =
    SUM(FactSales[Quantity])
```

- **Justification**: Total volume of units sold.
- **Business Question**: "How many units did we sell?"
- **Category**: Volume
- **Format**: #,0

#### Avg Transaction Value

```dax
[Avg Transaction Value] =
    DIVIDE(
        [Total Revenue],
        DISTINCTCOUNT(FactSales[SalesKey]),
        0
    )
```

- **Justification**: Average value per transaction. Upselling indicator.
- **Business Question**: "How much does a customer spend on average per purchase?"
- **Category**: Efficiency
- **Format**: $#,0.00

#### Customer Count

```dax
[Customer Count] =
    DISTINCTCOUNT(FactSales[FK_CustomerKey])
```

- **Justification**: Number of unique customers with at least one purchase.
- **Business Question**: "How many active customers do we have?"
- **Category**: Customer
- **Format**: #,0
